About Me

Throughout my career I have worked with hundreds of high school administrators and counselors in order to help students graduate college and career-ready.

At The City University of New York (CUNY), I managed a program that strengthened post-secondary guidance for first-generation students. I've since supported networks of New York City and Westchester county counselors in creating effective 9th through 12th grade college and career pathways. Additionally, I've worked within the CUNY and SUNY systems on deepening high school partnerships and increasing college retention and graduation rates.

I received my Bachelor’s degree in English Literature from Binghamton University, and my Master’s degree in Social Policy from SUNY Empire State College.
